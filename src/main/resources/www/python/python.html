The Jerk Cartel

The Jerk Cartel is a pirate alliance that exists and operates within the Massively Multiplayer Online Game of Eve-Online. The Jerk Cartel is made up of two corporations - The Python Cartel and Space Jerks. Our goal is to foster a cohesive group of pilots dedicated to the craft of piracy and player-versus-player combat.
